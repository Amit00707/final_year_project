Sanctuary Note

Our privacy framework is designed to protect your intellectual journey as fiercely as a locked library vault.

Privacy Policy

Effective: May 24, 2024 Version 4.2

1. Definitions

In this digital sanctuary, "Service" refers to the curated reading platform provided by The Scholarly Sanctuary. "User Content" encompasses the annotations, highlights, and personal reflections you entrust to our archives. We treat these artifacts not as data points, but as extensions of your intellectual property.

"Personal Information" includes any identifier that could bridge the gap between your digital persona and your physical identity. Our commitment is to maintain an impenetrable veil between these two worlds, ensuring your scholarly pursuits remain private.

2. Data Collection

We collect only the essential vellum required to maintain your experience. This includes session duration to optimize typography rendering and library organization preferences. Unlike commercial repositories, we do not harvest your reading speed or behavioral patterns for advertising syndication.

Your interactions with rare digital manuscripts are logged locally whenever possible. Syncing to our central archive is an opt-in ritual, reserved for those who wish to access their collection across multiple devices without sacrificing sovereignty.

3. AI Usage & Intelligence Policies

The AI Librarians within this Sanctuary are designed for Syntactic Enlightenment, not data mining. Unlike traditional models, our intelligence layer operates on a "Zero-Retention" protocol for all personal inquiries.

lock_reset Local Inference

Semantic analysis of your notes occurs on-device where hardware permits, minimizing network transit.

visibility_off Non-Training Mandate

Your unique reading insights are never used to train global models. Your wisdom remains yours.

When you request a "Deep Index" of a text, the AI processes the document in an ephemeral sandbox. Once the summary or insight is delivered, the sandbox is incinerated, leaving no digital residue of your specific query path.

4. Your Sovereignty

You hold the key to your data vault. At any moment, you may export your entire reading history in an open-standard format. Furthermore, the "Right to be Forgotten" in our sanctuary is absolute. Upon clicking the deletion ritual, every byte associated with your account is scrubbed from our primary and backup vellum stores within 72 hours.
