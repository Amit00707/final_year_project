About the Author

Evelyn St. Claire has spent four decades exploring the intersection of memory and landscape. Her prose is celebrated for its tactile quality, often described as "breathing life into the dust of history." Resident of the high moors, her work reflects the isolation and stark beauty of the British countryside.
